Species Profile

Basic Information

  • Name: Niandr.
    • Note: The Niandr do not have a name for their species, all names for them as a collective come from out-groups. Niandr is the Runi word for them, and is derived from the English word "neanderthal". It is the only word the Niandr recognize as refering to "all of us", despite its origin as an insulting comparison.
  • Scientific Classification/Taxonomy:
  • Common Names: Space Elves (Semi-affectionate), Dark Elves
  • Origin/Primary Habitat: Runa
  • Population Distribution: The Eastern continent of the planet Runa.

Niandr are a humanoid species with nearly 2 billion members primarily found within many nations on the continent of Shalora, and in one of four colonies on other continents. Their name was given to them by the Runi after learning that the human ancestor species was big, agressive, and "odd of mind". Humans typicaly call them some varient of "elf" largly due to their ear shape and extreme resemblance to humans.

The Niandr are native to Runa, and while they resemble humans there are some differences. A Niandrs height averages 73, with females standing around 711 and males around 610. Weight distribution does not follow the size difference with males averaging 320 lbs and females averaging 270 lbs. The difference in mass vs size is due to the Niandr Y chromosome carrying a gene sequence which creates far denser muscles than a Niandr Female can achieve.

A Niandr male is approximately 1.89x stronger than a human male of equal size and fitness. A Neander female is as strong as a human male of equal size and fitness. The sexes are effectively as strong as each other due to the females advantages in reach and leverage. The males strength comes into play primarily within the realm of labor.

Niandr are as intelligent as humans, though their size, strength, and mentality lead them to value some technologies less than humans do (They have less need and desire for mechanical labor saving devices.). The largest differences have to do with tribalism. While humans are capable of seeing hundreds or thousands of individuals as part of the tribe even if they act quite differently are are capable of putting themselves in their shoes of anyone they know, Niandr are not.

The Niandr experience extreme psychological distress if they attempt to think like someone who is a part of an outgroup. It is akin to an autistic meltdown, and is so severe it quickly brings on physical symptoms in the form of an acute migraine of debilitating strength. There are records of deaths occurring when individuals attempted to fight through the pain to understand.

There are drugs which can aid the Niandr in this area, though most find the concept too distressing to attempt even with medical aid. A small percentage of their population lacks this distress response and are seen as mentally disabled due to it.

In the last two hundred years monastic orders have been able to train individuals to see groups as individuals within a larger group through a nearly decade long process. These individuals serve as the Niandr's diplomats. Before these individuals existed, Niandr groups followed the ancient tradition of communicating through actions. In other words they would pretend out groups did not exist whenever possible and act as if only their tribes existed.

This system allowed the Niandr to develop into a technologically advanced species with many large nations with low-millions of citizens. Twice, as they bounced back after an accidental apocalypse. Niandr nations are composed of multiple tribes, who will use each others resources and infrastructure in a symbiotic relationship, all without communication. This would never work for any other species.

Even though there are now options for diplomacy the Niandr prefer to work around other tribes unless/until this becomes impossible. Their history is naturally filled with very bloody wars over extremely small conflicts simply due to a lack of desire to think of the other side as people.

This is not to imply they are a savage species, nor cold and unfeeling. The Niandr are a very empathetic people, as long as you appear to be a part of their tribe. This isn't racially oriented, but rather is behavioral. A human who studied a Niandr culture and simply imitated their accent, mannerisms, and common areas of interest would be seen as a member of the tribe by everyone within it. (This is how all diplomacy with Niandr and Terran cultures is to occur.)

A Niandr tribe's "common areas of interest" are a fascinating aspect of their species. It appears that some elements of their cultures are genetic, as each individual has one or two extreme interests in a broad field (such as stonework, farming, metalworking, fighitng, etc), and a dozen or so lesser yet still extreme interests in more niesh fields (Data entry, statistical analysis, organic chemistry, library sciences, etc). These interests are passed from parent to child, resulting in Niandr tribes having a "belt" of specialties they are extraordinarily skilled in.

Another mental quirk which shapes the Niandr as a species is something many humans cannot help but be extremely empathetic of. Niandr have difficulty remaining focused on single tasks as their minds are prone to wander and slight distractions can cause extreme disruptions to concentration. They are socially awkward, with individuals acting with reservation around most of their tribe save for one or two close friends who will share a bond that most species would see as that of mates due to their intensity. They have extreme difficulty with completing tedious labor, often requiring sources of entertainment to do such tasks even if they are simple in nature and quickly accomplished.

In brief, the normal neurotypical state for Niandr is Attention-Deficit/Hyperactivity Disorder, with a side of Autism Spectrum Disorder. They do have individuals who behave like neurotypical humans. These individuals are seen as mentally ill within their species and often medicated.

As the Niandr share their homeworld with another sapient species, their interactions with one another are important to understanding them. In short, the Niandr typically let the Runi "live wild" as they just do not care. Many groups see them as "animals" in the same sence as they see non-sapient life. This attitude isn't cultural racism, but is simply how they perceive any outgroup due to their neurological makeup. Many Niandr see other Niandr groups in the same way.

Typically this leads to a more advanced version of the "just ignore them" that is applied to other tribes. However, some Niandr tribes will "domesticate" Runi. This involves bringing them into their society, teaching them a trade, then pretty much leaving them to it, never to interact other than to take goods and leave pay. Most Runi see this as the closest thing Niandr can do to welcoming them into their tribe without the Runi changing their culture to match the tribes.

Only seven instances of Niandr enslaving Runi are found within recorded history. he most recent example being the Kingdom of Kairn, which was toppled by a slave revolt backed by several other Niandr and Runi nations (and possibly had assistance by the Terran Protectorate.)

Aside from those differences, the Niandr are close enough to human that they can be mistaken for tall, strong, humans. Many tribes, nations, and individuals are amicable towards others provided one sticks to that particular group's mannerisms and cultural keystones. Overall the species can be interacted with in a friendly manner provided one doesn't do anything to enrage a given group of Niandr.

Physical Characteristics

  • Average Height: 7'9"
  • Average Lifespan: 89 years
  • Build/Body Structure: humanoid
  • Skin/Fur/Scale Colors: grays, gray-blues, rarely white (like parchment)
  • Distinctive Features: Sunken eyes, naturally greasy hair.
  • Notable Abilities or Adaptations:
    • Physical Abilities: Exceptionally strong on their homeworld, which has 1.77g. Very dangerous in a human environment.
    • Sensory Abilities: Trinocular smell (Can smell with their nose and ears, letting them pinpoint any scent's source to within 4 meters.)
    • Environmental Adaptations: Only need 50% of their brain functioning to survive. As long as a given structure is intact within one hemisphere, a Niandr operates as if uninjured.

Biology and Physiology

  • Diet: Omniverious, prefers woody fibrous plants.
  • Reproduction: live birth.
  • Growth and Development: Human like, with delayed onset of old age.
  • Health and Healing: Niandr's sence of smell is so acute they have a fastidious need to keep their environments clean. Their industrial revolution was delayed by attempts to keep the process as odorless as possible.
  • Unique Physiological Traits: Niandr have small mineral accretions on their foreheads which resemble gems. These are fused to their skulls and grow naturally over time, emerging from the skin usually during puberty (can be before or after depending on diet). Males have two long thin gems over their eyebrows, females have one gem centered on their forehead. These gems serve no purpose other than storing consumed minerals which are toxic to the Niandr, making them functionally equivalent to an appendix. Most Niandr have their gem(s) polished or cut for ascetic purposes, as the color is unique to a tribe due to their shared food supply.
