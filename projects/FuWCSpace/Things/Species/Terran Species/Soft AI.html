Overview

Terran made AIs are special, culturally and technologically. While they take many forms in the physical and digital realmandroid brains, holographically projected personas, robot cores, or their true forms with a little extra massall Terran AIs from the technology's third generation onwards share a few commonalities.

  1. They are fully sapient lifeforms, able to learn and grow based on their interactions with their environment, choose their own actions based on internal knowledge of the world around them (Morals, ethics, physics, science, cultural and social norms, their self identity, all of these and more factor in.). They are in all senses of the word, people. They simply have a creator-given purpose which provides them joy and fulfillment to engage in (but are free to do otherwise).
    1. They are considered Terran citizens for this reason. Especially if manufactured by the Protectorate directly.
  2. They typically desire some form of body that can interact with their fellow Terrans more easily than they can with their natural bodies due to size and occasionally viscosity reasons.
  3. There are two Taxa of AI: Hard and Soft.
    1. Soft AI are are cybernetic creations consisting of hardware nanomachines suspended in a slime-mold-based organic-gel matrix.
    2. Hard AI are pure hardware creations typically permanently integrated into whatever they were created to manage. They are much more rare than Soft AI as their creation is seen as an act of crusty by many Terrans due to their lack of mobility. Equivalent to intentionally giving a child full body paralysis at birth.

This entry covers Soft AI due to the rarity of Hard AI as well as Hard AI primarily existing as unique custom creations with no standard components.

Functionality

Terran AIs are gelatinous semi-organic "soft robots" which can subsist entirely on electricity. They are able to connect wirelessly or via direct contact to any compatible device capable of housing their mass. This can be a robot, android, starship, building, or anything in between. They are able to be poured into and out of different platforms as needs require.

They are also motile to a small degree, able to reform themselves into different shapes to move, fill a device's core, or manipulate their environment. While the original intention was to allow them to be as mobile for emergency situations where they may need to escape a damaged core AIs quickly created systems allowing them to imitate human shapes, albeit in miniature, so they could do anything they wanted (at scale).

Each AI is equivalent to a human mind in most respects, but is imbued with a "purpose" aka a specialization which allows them to function as the computer system they are when engaging with that particular field / operation. This allows them to interact with humans and human society comfortably and without complications of other systems. Effectively, each AI is a manufactured savant.

Presence Bodies

Thus far, all AI have wanted one or more "Presence Bodies", a physical form they can control directly, be that remotely or in person, which enables them to physically interact with the organic world as an organic would. These bodies typically take one of four forms, with an AI's preferred form being entirely personal choice.

Cyber Gel (but bigger)

Some AIs prefer their body be augmented with additional cybernetic gel. Enough to allow them the size of a shorter human. They can them form their body into any shape they please (Typically they mimic another Terran race) while remaining "What I am", to quote most AIs with this preference.

This form is only possible for AIs who work with systems that have larger than normal cores. Such as those who aid in the operations of starships, starbases, buildings, factories, etc.

Robot

Many AIs prefer a robotic form of some kind. Many enjoy humanoid chassis, but all manner of platforms are in wide use. Some AIs with this preference maintain a small fleet of bodies so they can hop from one shell to another to use whatever is best for a given activity.

Android

A large number of AIs like to be as close to an organic as possible. Others like to look a lot like an organic while remaining clearly robotic. This is a personal choice and leads to them adopting an android body with a specific appearance unique to them. This is effectively their body form then on, with them using remote links to operate any system they are required to use rather than slip into another device's core.

As this can be achieved with negligible latency, its seen as fine for any setup where the tiny delay wont cause an issue.

Avatar

Many AIs prefer to remain within the digital realm. To this end they remain within a core somewhere in the physical world and construct a digital avatar to represent themselves with, almost exactly like a person would when playing a videogame. These avatars can be anything at all, but are usually a stylized representation of an organic species, or an abstract representation thereof, or a polygonal creation only possible within cyberspace.

These AI make use of a small remote drone equipped with a hard light holographic projector and a gravitic float system to interact with the physical world. Many of them prefer to stylize their projections rather than aim for photorealism. Monochromatic projections, achromatic aberration, scanlines, digital "glitching", and other artistic flares are common.

Generations

There are five generations of sapient AI. The generations are less about technological seniority and more about specialization. All generations remain in production.

  • Gen 3: The original sapient AI system. Produced when a general purpose AI is needed.
  • Gen 4: Cybernetic interface modules of nanites optimized for controlling many machines in unison. Intended for use in situations where swarm robotics are applied.
  • Gen 5: Gel-matrix made more flexible to easily and comfortably fill complex "pipe" "nest" type core systems often found in industrial and scientific machinery.
  • Gen 6: A small solid core was added to produce new nanites, and the nanites were upgraded to be capable of ingesting organic material. Both to allow them to repair themselves in the event of damage. Intended for use in combat and hazardous environments.
  • Gen 7: Greatly increased radio resistance. Intended for use in space, but also utilized in maintenance of certain power plants and cleanup of radioactive materials.

Flesh Slime

Flesh Slime is a form of technology related to the gel matrix of Soft AI. It is a purely biotech creation using organic cellular machinery in place of nanomachines. its function is primarily to consume available materials and create biological or technological items. It is not a common technology.
