Unda Profile

Basic Information

  • Name: Unda
  • Scientific Classification/Taxonomy: undaascensor concentio
  • Common Names: Waveform Striders
  • Origin/Primary Habitat: Ade
  • Population Distribution: 42 star systems in a constellation surrounding the XO-4 system.

The Unda are a mysterious species with a small Star-Empire located around XO-4. They are not expansionist and appear content with their currently occupied territory. They have minimal interactions with the Terran Protectorate with the most notable events being the Undanese Pirate Counter-Incursion and the so-called "War of the Bands".

So little is known about them that their name is a placeholder given to them by human explorers due to their resemblance to water striders, an old Earth species, along with their extremely obvious obsessive love of music. Their star-empire's name, the Techno Union is likewise a human creation. It is in reference to an ancient popculture in-joke and stuck due to a piece of technology ubiquitous to Undanese ships, planets, stations, and presumably everywhere they inhabit; the spacetime oscillator.

A spacetime oscillator is technology Terrans do not have, do not understand, and cannot currently speculate upon. Through its unknown means it is able to induce vibrations within the fabric of spacetime itself. Unlike gravplating or spacial warp based FTL systems, the warping of space is able to be preformed nearly instantly in any given direction and to large degrease. In theory a spacetime oscillator could be used to set gravity in cosmologically significant region to arbitrary values.

This is not how they are used by the Unda. Like, at all.

The Unda make use of this technology to continuously vibrate space in large radiuses around their vessels, stations, and inhabited worlds to transform the void itself into a sound system. This forces every object within the effected area to transform into a speaker. From which they play music which most closely resembles techno.

Constantly.

This music is played at such high volume and intensity it acts as a destructive barrier to anything approaching their inhabited spaces, causing it to shake apart. (They can and do make use of "sound lasers" to direct beams of concentrated space-oscillating bass as their form of ship-to-ship weapons, though this is clearly a secondary use case.)

This is not the intended function.

Remote observation of Undanese worlds shows they like to dance. At least, their bouncing-motions up and down in time to the bass track of their music is assumed to be a dance. In other words, they play their music for enjoyment so loud you can hear it from an AU away. Through the vacuum of space. How they can function, and their own equipment doesn't crumble, within such an environment is unknown.
